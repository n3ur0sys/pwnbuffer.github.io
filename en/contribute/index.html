Do you have an article about hacking or cybersecurity that you’d like to share with our audience? We are open to receiving quality content to enrich our project. Here’s how to submit:

Submission Guidelines: Link to heading

  1. Format: Submit your article in Markdown (.md) format.
  2. Originality: The content must be original and cannot have been published elsewhere.
  3. Structure: Use headings and subheadings to organize the text. Properly indent the content to facilitate reading.
  4. Images: If you have images, submit them separately and include proper sources if necessary.
  5. References: Include all references used in the article, with links or full details.
  6. Biography: Include a brief biography of yourself for credits.

How to Submit: Link to heading

  • Create a ticket in our Discord and submit your article.

After submission, our team will review it and get in touch if your article is approved for publication.
